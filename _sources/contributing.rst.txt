============
Contributing
============

Thank you for your interest in ``souffle-lint``! We welcome and appreciate all
kinds of contributions.

You may want to take a look at:

- The developer guide, specifically

  * :doc:`build`

  * :doc:`dev`

- The `issues labeled "good first issue" <good_>`_

- The `issues labeled "help wanted" <help_>`_

If you have questions, please ask them on the `discussions page <discuss_>`_!
